# Exercise Walkthroughs

These exercises walk you through practical AI workflows you can use in your daily work. They are designed for self-paced learning -- work through them at your own speed, experiment, and adapt the patterns to your needs.

## Exercises

- **[Build a PowerPoint](/ai-learning-pct/docs/exercises/powerpoint)** - Use AI + templates to generate consistent presentations fast
- **[Research Brief](/ai-learning-pct/docs/exercises/research-brief)** - Generate a structured research brief on any topic using AI

## What You'll Learn

Each exercise demonstrates a core pattern:

1. **Template + AI = consistent output** - Encode your standards in a template, let AI apply them to new content
2. **Research + AI = fast synthesis** - Get to an 80% draft fast, then apply your expertise to finish it
3. **Iterate, don't expect perfection** - Generate a first draft, review, refine

## Prerequisites

- GitHub Copilot CLI (`ghcp`) installed and configured
- Access to the templates directory (from copilot-dotfiles)
- Terminal open in your copilot-dotfiles directory
