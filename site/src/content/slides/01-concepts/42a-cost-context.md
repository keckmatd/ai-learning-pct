## What Does This Cost?

**Order of magnitude (Claude Opus):**
- ~$15 per 1 million input tokens
- ~$75 per 1 million output tokens

**A typical research brief?**
~50-100K tokens in, a few thousand out — call it **a few dollars**.

**A team member using it all day, every day?**
Tens of dollars a month. Maybe a hundred on a heavy month.

> Your team's monthly AI cost will likely come in under one lunch meeting.

The question isn't "can we afford it?" — it's "what's the cost of *not* using it?"
