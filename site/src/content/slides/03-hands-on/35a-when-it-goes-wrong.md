## When It Goes Wrong

**Real example:** AI generated a research brief but cited a policy document that doesn't exist.

**Recovery steps:**

1. **Always verify citations** — click the link, check the source
2. **Ask AI to check its own sources** — "Verify each citation actually exists"
3. **Iterate** — "That source seems wrong, find the actual policy"

> Failure is part of the workflow, not a bug.
