## You Control What AI Accesses

With CLI AI running locally, **you** set the boundaries:

- **Permissions model** -- tools only run when you approve them
- **Audit trail** -- every command, every file read is logged in your terminal
- **Data residency** -- your code stays on your machine until you decide otherwise
- **No surprise uploads** -- nothing leaves without an explicit network call you can inspect

> As a government team, this matters: your data, your rules, your machine.
