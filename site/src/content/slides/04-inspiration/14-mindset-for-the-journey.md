## Mindset for the Journey

### Start Small
Don't try to build keck_companion next week. 
- First: use what you have
- Then: customize for your needs
- Later: build new capabilities

### Iterate
Your first instructions will be wrong. 
Your first templates will be rough.
That's normal. Improve as you go.

### Share
- What works for you might help others
- What confuses you probably confuses others
- The group gets better together

### Be Patient
This is a new skill. Give it real time.
- Week 1: Awkward
- Month 1: Useful sometimes
- Month 3: Part of your workflow
- Month 6: Can't imagine working without it
