## Your Homework (Optional but Encouraged)

### This Week
1. **Finish installation** if you didn't wrap it up today — the install guide in the docs site walks you through it
2. **Use CLI AI for one real task** — draft a memo, summarize a policy doc, or outline a brief
3. **Keep a friction log** — note one thing that was awkward; bring it to the Slack channel

### This Month
1. **Edit your `copilot-instructions.md`** — add your role, your tone, three things AI should always know about your work
2. **Build one template** — take a document you produce weekly (status update, meeting notes) and turn it into a reusable template
3. **Create one skill** for a workflow you repeat — even a 10-line skill saves time every time you use it

### This Quarter
1. **Share one win in `#ai-practitioners`** — a prompt, template, or skill that worked
2. **Pair with someone** — 30 minutes watching a colleague use AI teaches more than hours of docs
3. **Decide what sticks** — which pieces of this are actually part of your workflow now? Double down there
