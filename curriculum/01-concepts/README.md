## Teaching Approach

Start each topic with what they already know from ChatGPT/Gemini, then reveal the mechanics underneath.

**Pattern**:
1. "You've probably noticed..." (connect to experience)
2. "Here's what's actually happening..." (reveal mechanism)
3. "This matters because..." (practical implication)

---

## Key Transitions

After tokens: "So if AI sees pieces, not meaning... how does it understand?"

After context: "So if there's a limit... what happens in long conversations?"

After RAG: "So AI can look things up... but where does it store what it learns?"

After memory: "So it doesn't really remember... how do we make it persistent?"

After MCP: "Now you understand the machinery. Let's talk about building with it."
